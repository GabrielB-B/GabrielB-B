## About Me

I work in the financial sector of the Procuradoria-Geral do Estado and have spent the last 3 years dealing directly with routines that demand control, traceability, accuracy, and operational safety.

My path in tech has been built around real problems instead of theoretical exercises. Projects such as `automacao-rpvs` and `siscon-controle-rpv` came from concrete needs in my work environment and helped improve not only my own workflow, but also the routine of the team around me.

- In `automacao-rpvs`, I automated the issuance of judicial guides from large spreadsheets of legal processes, replacing a repetitive one-by-one manual workflow in the portal.
- In `siscon-controle-rpv`, I built a structured system and database layer that improved control in multiple fronts, especially by preventing duplicate payments and making the process much safer.
- In `Cesta_Digital`, I applied the same practical mindset to a real community need in church, building a solution with direct social value.

I currently study Analysis and Systems Development at UNIT and keep sharpening my skills through constant hands-on practice, including project-based study routines. I also completed part of an Actuarial Science degree, which strengthened my analytical view of finance, data, and process control.

What motivates me most is creating useful applications with real impact: systems that remove friction, reduce manual work, and turn messy operational routines into something clearer, safer, and more reliable.
